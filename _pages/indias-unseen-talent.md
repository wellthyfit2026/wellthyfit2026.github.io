We, at Wellthy.fit, have taken the initiative of unearthing and bringing to the forefront of attention some of those top class athletes that haven’t received their due yet – poor fame and scanty funding! These are the athletes who continue to pursue, toil and excel in their sport for the love of it, despite poor infrastructure, zilch support from government and private organizations-simply because they have not been able to reach out.


Through journalists based in various regions, we reach out to athletes with powerful achievements.  Through our intervention we spread their good work on social media and contact agencies with the hope to find them suitable and full-fledged support to help them prepare for their upcoming international events.
