WellthyFit’s mission is to add energy and spark to people’s fitness journey no matter what level of fitness they are at! Through inspiring videos, motivational quotes, real life stories, practical advice, right products, we want to bring fitness to you in a non-judgmental, fun and inclusive way! Right from running to yoga to health food to meditation to mind body connection to finding the right workout classes and fitness apparel, books and products, our mission is to explore fitness in all its vigor.

Through our blogs, we wish to help you staying the course for life which is the real challenge. We are happy to consult and bring in the right people to you who can coach you!  As very outspoken fitness advocates, fitness gatherers and without being extremists, we are dedicated to help people start and maintain a deliberately fit life!
