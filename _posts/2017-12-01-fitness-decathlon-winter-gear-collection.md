### You never come back empty handed from a [Decathlon](https://www.decathlon.in/)store!


### Try! And you will fail!


The key to an active winter is in your right fitness clothes! And happiness for an active person is to be in a space that inspires her to start an active life without having to spend a bomb! Decathlon is the favorite hangout for people who love sports, are trying a new sport, and want to spend time with their children talking about sports! Why:


1. It has gear and equipment for every possible indoor and outdoor sport.

2. Great pricing. Almost Budget.

3. Kids, women, men - there is everything for all three.

4. The stores are large. There is energy. And you see people even trying to Bike inside.

5. They have the best sales people - because they are passionate about sports themselves.


#### **In short - Decathalon keeps it simple. It is all about SPORTS!**


I was at the Decathlon store in Huda City Centre last Sunday. And their winter stuff is worth a dekko and pick, stocked with great winter fitness essentials. So if you are in Gurgaon, at the Huda City Centre, Do walk in at the Big Big Decathalon!
