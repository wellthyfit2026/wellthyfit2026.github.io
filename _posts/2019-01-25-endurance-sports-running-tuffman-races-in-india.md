*The green hills, the snow captured peaks, the Victorian-era architecture, the silent roads of the township, he suddenly felt a nostalgia for Shimla. His tea had gobbled three pieces of Nutrichoice biscuits and he was tripped down the memory lane. Suddenly, a voice came “Dad! Half Marathon this time! I am going to run 21 kilometers.”*


*His son was a newbie in field of running and the excitement was getting reflected on his face. But he was an ultra runner**who knew running is a world of its own, recruiting and strengthening more muscles and the mental side which lacks in other workouts. Suddenly, he heard the notification sound of his new iPhone. “ TUFFMAN SHIMLA ULTRA MASHOBRA RACE”  Wow! It would be fantastic if I get to run with my son that too at the trails and heights at Shimla?”*


![](/assets/images/2019/01/logo-resized.jpg)


*And so here we are  - with a name that stands out in the endurance running scene in India – [TUFFMAN](http://www.tuffmanindia.com/about.html)- bringing running, cycling and swimming events to Ultra sports enthusiasts.Founded by Sanjay Mangla in 2013,  Tuffman promises to deliver Exceptional Experiences by waking up the tough man that resides inside everyone and implanting that toughness in mind.*


*As a community - Tuffman’s endeavour is to inspire amateur athletes and fill them with an urge to achieve more by awarding them with a name of “Tuffman”.*


![](/assets/images/2019/01/Low-Res-Tuffman-Moonson-Ultra-Coorg.jpg)


![](/assets/images/2019/01/Low-Res-Tuffman-Ladakh-Duathlon.jpg)


***HOW  DOES IT STAND UNIQUE? WHY TUFFMAN? WHAT IS ITS USP?***


- *Taking your running to amazing places, Tuffman makes an effort to involve every terrain starting from island at Andaman to athletic track at Chandigarh, from snow at Manali to fine Tarmac at Gurugram, from Trail at Shimla to Heights at Ladakh, from Beach at Goa to Speed Tarmac at Chandigarh to Winters at Kasauli and finally to Deserts and Dunes at Jaisalmer.*

- *Encouraging runners to come out of cliched road running to these locations with families & friends making it a family & friendship driven fitness vacation.*


***Pick an exotic location where Tuffman has taken many runners to and get – Tuffman-ed!***
